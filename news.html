Overlay introduces Precision AR

×
Overlay’s solution is based on the close cooperate with Leica Geosystem and Geographic Information Systems provider. However, there have some problems in the using of augmented reality technology outdoors like uncontrollable scene slippage and inaccurate data representation. Although reliability and accuracy of the software still need improvements, the publish of the software have opened new possibilities for the use of AR in the field.
The technology enables site drawings to be created in the field using only a mobile device combined with a satellite receiver that can then be automatically generated and edited via CAD software.Overlay states that GIS data collection and correction can be done in a fraction of the time and at a higher degree of accuracy, which will help to change how businesses manage their infrastructure assets.
